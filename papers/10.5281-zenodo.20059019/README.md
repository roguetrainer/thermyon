# Volume of Thought (VoT) and Topological Agentic Swarms: Scaling Multi-Agent AI from Temporal Concurrency to Geometric Contradiction via the MGE Router

**Paper:** 213 | **Portfolio C** — AI & Enterprise

**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.20059019](https://zenodo.org/records/20059019)

## Abstract

Introduces VoT: a taxonomy of agentic AI reasoning topologies (Chain, Tree, Mesh, Simplicial). The MGE router replaces softmax attention to enforce the Excluded Volume Principle, preventing contradictory agent shards from co-occupying a Fano face.
