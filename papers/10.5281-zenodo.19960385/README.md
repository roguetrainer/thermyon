# Nuclear Magic Numbers and Exceptional Lie Algebras

**Paper:** 245 | **Portfolio E** — Grand Challenges

**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.19960385](https://zenodo.org/records/19960385)

## Abstract

Proposes that nuclear magic numbers (2, 8, 20, 28, 50, 82, 126) have a geometric explanation in exceptional Lie algebra representation theory via the R-C-H-O division algebra ladder.
