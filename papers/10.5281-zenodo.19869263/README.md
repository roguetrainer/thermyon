# The Fano-Foam Manifold and the Excluded Volume Principle

**Paper:** 200 | **Portfolio B** — Foundational Mathematics

**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.19869263](https://zenodo.org/records/19869263)

## Abstract

Introduces the Fano-Foam Manifold: a simplicial complex built from Fano-line 2-simplices. The Excluded Volume Principle states that two facts sharing a non-Fano triple cannot co-occupy the same simplex — the geometric basis of logical consistency in the ASA.
