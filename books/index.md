Multi-page works in two registers: *stories* (narrative, character-driven) and *primers* (tutorial, example-driven). Both use the same framework. Both are written for a reader who knows their own field well but is new to the C/T lens.

---

**[Professor Knot Stories](professor-knot-stories)** — Topology, thermodynamics, and the secret unity of everything, told through the eyes of Vera Knot, Otto Gibbs (a bear), Dr. Felix (a cat), and Marina. Written in the tradition of Gamow's *Mr Tompkins*.

**[CT Chemistry Primer](ct-chemistry-primer)** — High-school and first-year chemistry through the C/T lens. Side-by-side classic and C/T methods. Six chapters from H₂ to nitrogen fixation.

**The Little Origamist** — *In preparation.* A novel. A child discovers that the rules of paper-folding are the same rules that govern how molecules work, how computers think, and why some problems are hard.
