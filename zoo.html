A catalogue of physical, chemical, biological, and mathematical processes expressed as programmes in the Origami Instruction Set Architecture.

Each entry names the symmetry group, specifies the interpretation functor, gives the explicit opcode sequence, and produces a computable output. Entries marked validated have computed outputs confirmed against experiment or established theory.
